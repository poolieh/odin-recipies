Lasagna Recipe

Everybody, not only orange cats, love lasagna. Here is an easy to cook recipie for homemade lasagna to make your mondays more surviable..

At first, you should cook the sauce properly.

Then you put the sauce on the noodle-plates.

Finally, you can put your lasagna into the oven.

Ingridients

  • 250g tomato sauce
  • 100g minced meat
  • 5 lasagna plates
