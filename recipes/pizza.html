Pizza Recipe

A good pizza is easy to make but very hard to master. Here is a recipie that you can start with.

At first, you should make your pizza-dough.

Secondly you put your tomato sauce and cheese on the pizza.

Finally you can put your pizza in the oven.

Ingridients

  • 200g pizza dough
  • 100g tomato sauce
  • 50g mozerella-cheese
